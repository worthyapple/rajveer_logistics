Courier Shipping Bill

A shipping bill is a legal document used in international trade and shipping to facilitate the movement of goods across international borders. It is typically required for customs clearance and for complying with various international regulations and trade agreements. A courier shipping bill, sometimes referred to as a courier waybill or courier invoice, is a specific type of shipping bill used by courier companies to ship parcels and documents.
