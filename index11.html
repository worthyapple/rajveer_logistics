Frequently asked questions

Parcel Exchange Center

What is Local Delivery Franchise?

The program is an extension of the Rajveer pick-up and delivery network. We seek entrepreneurs willing to run their own parcel and freight pick-up and delivery business.

Who can become a Local Delivery Franchise partner?

Anyone with the ability to set up basic necessary infrastructure and staffing to manage pick-up and delivery operations and with an understanding of their local geography is eligible to become a partner.

How long does the application process take?

Between 12 to 15 days. After a thorough evaluation process, Rajveer picks a Partner who is appointed exclusively to the location of the partner's choice.

Does it cost anything to become a partner for Rajveer?

Yes. If you choose to join the programme, you'll need to provide a security deposit amount ranging from ₹ 2-5 lakhs (refundable without interest) or a bank guarantee. If you're a professional in the delivery business, it’s likely that you already have the necessary infrastructure in place. If not, setting up the necessary requirements might incur initial start-up costs.

Will training be provided?

Yes, Rajveer will provide virtual and physical training. You'll also have access to online training content for future reference.

What are the daily operations like?

Daily operations involve adhering to basic operational processes- receiving bags at centers, sorting of parcels, dispatching them to customers for successful deliveries along with reverse pick-ups from customers. Additionally, daily compliance on cash deposition and audits are mandatory. All status updates will be monitored using our technology platform.

How does a partner get paid?

Payments are done on a per-parcel basis, every fortnight. The Rajveer team raises invoices, and upon acceptance by the partner, payments are processed further.

How many areas/city/state can a partner cover?

This varies by the partner. Reliable, long-term partners are encouraged to expand serviceability and take on larger areas.

What is the tenure of the agreement?

This will be a permanent agreement, which is revised and renewed annually. Rajveer retains the right to terminate a contract with a partner subject to the partner not meeting required performance and compliance standards.

Rajveer Courier Store

Local Delivery Franchise vs Courier & Sales Franchise

In the Local Delivery Franchise program, a partner sets up a last mile delivery center and delivers all the shipments to customers in his/her location. Rajveer drops the parcels at the partner's last mile center. Partner gets paid for the delivery services. In the Courier & Sales Franchise program, a partner sets up a company branded retail counter and books the parcels and documents of walkin customers and businesses. Rajveer picks up shipments from partners' retail counters and delivers them. Partner gets commission on all shipping charges and other value added services.

What infrastructure is needed to set up a Rajveer courier store?

A storefront on the main road of a premium location with good visibility and a lot of business activity. The storefront has to be at least 80 sqft (8ftx10ft) and can go up to 200 sqft. The interiors and branding have to be done as per company guidelines and the work will be done by one of the company's authorized partners.

Is there an investment involved?

Yes. The storefront setup will cost anywhere from Rs. 1.5L to 3L depending on the city and outlet floor area. There will be a 100% refundable security deposit of Rs. 3L to 10L depending on the location and city of the outlet. There will also be a one time non-refundable registration fee of Rs. 50,000 that covers the account setup, training to the franchise and startup kit to start the business.

How can I earn by joining Rajveer courier franchise program?

Franchise partners earn commission on all the retail counter bookings and also make profits by providing domestic and international shipping solutions to businesses around them.

What happens if a customer's parcel is lost?

Rajveer will compensate the customer for any loss or damage to the extent covered by the liability clause. The same is subject to our terms and conditions. The cover increases if the customer opted for insurance while booking the parcel or document.

How do I book the shipments?

Our partner support team will provide a detailed training session on all our products and booking panel as part of our onboarding process. We also publish frequent training material for the same.
